Wildcard Scholarship Criteria

Without a Master's degree, you must apply for a wildcard scholarship. These are extremely rare. Do you meet at least 3 of these criteria?

Our Research Areas

Your research experience should be relevant to at least one of these areas - either by subject matter or by methods/techniques you use (e.g., optimization, machine learning).

  • Battery energy storage systems: optimal sizing, operation, and aggregation for grid applications
  • Electric vehicle integration: charging optimization, V2G, and fleet management
  • Mining/industrial electrification: battery-electric vehicles and hybrid renewable systems
  • Demand response and flexibility aggregation: VPP operation and prosumer coordination
  • Artificial intelligence and machine learning for power systems forecasting and optimization
  • Electricity market participation: bidding strategies and revenue optimization
  • Community batteries and local energy markets: sizing, operation, and business models
  • Smart grid technologies: large-scale data analytics, data compression, and prosumer privacy

Also check our recent publications to get a better picture of ongoing research.

Why Join Our Research Group?

  • Work on cutting-edge projects with tangible real-world impact in renewable energy and electrification
  • Access to state-of-the-art HPC facilities and industry partnerships with major organizations
  • Opportunities for industry placements and international collaborations
  • Strong track record of research impact by conventional and non-conventional metrics
  • Pathway to commercialization through spin-offs like OptiGrid Pty Ltd
  • Supportive environment with proven supervision track record
  • Competitive funding packages with the possibility of top-up scholarships and research allowances

What We Offer

  • Competitive Australian Government Research Training Program (RTP) scholarships
  • Top-up scholarships from industry partners (up to $10K p.a. additional funding)
  • Research allowances for conferences, equipment, and travel ($5K+ p.a.)
  • Industry-PhD programs with additional benefits and guaranteed industry placements
  • International placement opportunities (e.g., 6-month placements in Denmark with flights and stipend)
  • Access to Adelaide Power System Summer School intensive training
  • Collaborative research environment with regular group meetings and workshops
  • Opportunities to co-author papers with international collaborators
