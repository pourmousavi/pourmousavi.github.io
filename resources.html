Danish Residential Prosumers Dataset

Scientific Data, 2023

Synthetic dataset of five types of Danish residential prosumers' imported and exported electricity data. Developed using real consumer data, PV generation from GSEE model, EV charging data from emobpy, residential ESS operator, and GAN-based synthetic data generation.

R. Yuan, S. A. Pourmousavi, W. L. Soong, A. J. Black, J. A. R. Liisberg, J. Lemos-Vinasco

Prosumer Smart Grid Synthetic Data Denmark

MILES-SA

Mixed Integer Linear Energy Storage

MILP-based model to calculate energy storage requirements for South Australia to achieve 100% renewable electricity. Includes Copper Plate (single node) and Multi Nodal (7 nodes + 3 interconnectors) grid models using AEMO public data.
